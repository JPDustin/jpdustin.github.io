Your feedback is essential to improving our local union. Please take a few minutes to complete this survey.

Survey Overview

We are conducting a climate survey to gather input on the current state of our local union. This survey is anonymous, and your responses will directly influence our future actions and strategies.

This survey has been created and is being conducted by a neutral member of Local 633. The questions are carefully designed to provide a comprehensive assessment of the current climate within our union.

How the Results Will Be Used

The results of this survey will be analyzed to identify areas where our union can improve and grow. This data will help us better understand the needs and concerns of our members, ensuring that our actions reflect the collective will of the union.
