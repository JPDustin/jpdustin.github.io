Our Mission

The 633 Teamster Power Slate is committed to providing exceptional representation to all our members with unwavering diligence, integrity, and transparency from day one. We advocate for our members with a strong, assertive, and proactive approach. Our mission is to vigorously defend the rights and interests of our members in all matters, from workplace issues to negotiations and disputes. We believe in taking decisive action to protect and advance our members' interests, unifying and empowering them through dedicated representation.

If elected, we aim to achieve the following within Local 633:

  • Strengthen Member Representation: Ensure all members receive prompt and effective support in grievances, negotiations, and disputes.
  • Enhance Communication: Maintain open, transparent, and consistent communication with all members, ensuring they are informed and engaged.
  • Promote Member Involvement: Encourage active participation from members in union activities, fostering a stronger and more united community.
  • Advocate for Fair Contracts: Negotiate robust contracts that prioritize members' needs and secure better wages, benefits, and working conditions.
